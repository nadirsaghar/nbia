Viewing Image Animation
From the Search Results (Images for Series) (DICOM Series) page, you can launch an animation of thumbnails where there are more than two images in the series. Click the View Series in Cine Mode link.
You can also launch the animation of an image series from the Data Basket. In the View column of the Data Basket, click the magnifying glass symbol, ( corresponding to the series you want to view. On the View Series Images page, click the View Series in Cine Mode link.
When the viewer opens, click the play forward button (. NBIA proceeds to display the images sequentially in a “movie”.
