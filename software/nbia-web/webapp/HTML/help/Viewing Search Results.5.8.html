Adding Items to Your Basket
You can add items to your basket at the subject [patient], study, and series levels.
1.
To select items, use the Check All and Uncheck All buttons above the search results tables to select or deselect all items on a given search results page or click the corresponding check boxes for items individually.
Check boxes do not exist on the Images for Series page. If you click the Add to Basket button on the page, all of the images in the series are added to the Data Basket.
2.
When satisfied, click the Add to Basket button.
The items added to the basket are highlighted on the page from which you initiated this task.
